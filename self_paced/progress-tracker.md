## Suggested File Habit

Keep one working portfolio using [portfolio-template.md](portfolio-template.md). After each unit, add the unit deliverable and one sentence about what you verified.

## When To Move On

Move to the next unit when you can answer:

- What source evidence did I use?
- What did I ask Codex to produce?
- What privacy boundary did I apply?
- What did I verify?
- What remains uncertain?
